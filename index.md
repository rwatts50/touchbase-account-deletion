TouchBase allows users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

## How to request deletion
To request deletion of your TouchBase account and data, email:

support@thesoftwaretub.co.uk

from the email address associated with your TouchBase account.

## What data is deleted
When a deletion request is processed, the following data is permanently removed:
- User account information
- Emergency contacts
- Check-in schedules
- Activity history

## Processing time
Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

If you have questions, contact support@thesoftwaretub.co.uk
